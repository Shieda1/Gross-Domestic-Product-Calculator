What is the gross domestic product calculator?
The gross domestic product calculator is a tool that calculates the aggregate domestic output produced in a given country in a nominal term.
How to calculate gross domestic product?
To calculate gross domestic product you should use the following formula:
gross domestic product = consumption + investment + government purchases + exports - imports
